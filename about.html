Who We Are

UB Multipurpose Technology Company Limited is not your typical tech firm. We are a collection of engineers, researchers, and visionaries based in Enugu State, Nigeria, dedicated to creating proprietary intellectual property.

We realized that for technology to truly transform society, it must be simplified and built specifically for the environment it serves. We stopped building for others and started building for the future.

Our Mission

Our Promise

Every line of code and every circuit we design is original. We guarantee cutting-edge reliability that empowers users across all sectors.
